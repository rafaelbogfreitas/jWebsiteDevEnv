MEET J

J is a London based singer songwriter. J music is organic and sophisticated with a tropical twist. From uplifting acoustic songs to thoughtful lyrics ballads. He's here to enjoy himself and uplift others with the timeless message of Peace&Love.

He wrote, composed, arranged and performed all his songs. He also had a valuable collaboration by some of the most talented British musicians, Basil Isaacs on percussions, Dan Boardman on Trombone, Tom Harrison and Miguel Gorodi on Trumpets, Abraham Hampton on piano and keyboards from Harlem US and Paulo Santos on Drums.
